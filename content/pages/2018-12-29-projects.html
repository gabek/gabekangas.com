#TheCabin Mobile


  • Description: #TheCabin is a 15+ year old online chat community founded and maintained by Gabe.

  • #TheCabin's mobile client is an iPhone Application build for two reasons 1) To allow brand new people a quick way to join the IRC channel without worrying about servers or what software to use and 2) To give people who frequent the channel features and functionality that are useful to them.
  • Status: Version 2 is a complete native Objective-C rewrite launched in December 2012.   Version 1 was a hybrid application primarily written in Javascript.

AllReads

AllReads

AllReads attacks the problem of "Read It Later and Forget".

Utilizing the Pocket.com "Read it Later" service it reminds you at user-defined times utilizing multiple channels to let you know that you have unread items that you specifically wanted to get back to.

Lovify

  • A small OS X utility to allow you to "Love" a song that you're listening to on Spotify with the social music service "Last.FM".

PandoraFM


  • Description: PandoraFM is a web application created to allow you to sync your Pandora.com and Last.FM online radio experiences.
  • Status: Version 3 of PandoraFM that includes artist information, custom Last.FM radio player, and Youtube and Flickr integration has been running with very little code change. As of this writing over 8 million tracks have been played through the PandoraFM interface. Many still rely on the service daily, but it's unlikely there will be any additional updates in order to stay away from "feature creep".
