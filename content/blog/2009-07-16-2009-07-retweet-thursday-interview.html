Well who would have thought?  The fine people over at Silicon Prairie News were interested in talking to me about the weirdest meme to ever hit Twitter (aside from #MufasaMonday I suppose), #retweet_thursday. They told me they weren't sure who was behind it at first, but figured out it was out of Omaha somewhere, so they pinpointed it to me.  I never really made it public that the bot was run by me, but I wasn't really hiding the fact either :)

They came to me asking if I'd be willing to tell my story about why it came about, what the deal with the @RT_Thursday account was, and how it all worked.
